Dr. Adarsh Patel

Dr. Sajjad Ahmed

Sajjad Ahmed received a Masters of Technology degree in communication and information technology from the National Institute of Technology Srinagar (NIT Srinagar), Jammu and Kashmir, India, in 2011. He has submitted his Ph.D. thesis in computer engineering with the Dr. Zakir Hussein College of Engineering and Technology, Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, India. He received the prestigious Visvesvaraya Ph.D. fellowship from the Ministry of Electronics and IT (MeitY), Government of India, for pursuing his Ph.D. He has more than 7 years of teaching experience as an assistant professor at NIT Srinagar and TEQIP-III, a World Bank and MHRD project. He has also received research grants from AICTE. He has publications in several peer-reviewed international journals. He is an open-source enthusiast, advocate, and active user of open-source software. He is also a reviewer for many international conferences and journals. His areas of interest in study include cyber security, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and multimedia forensics
